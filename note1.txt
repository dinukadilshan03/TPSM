The relationship between predictors and Y is linear.(since we want to use mulitple regression)

===========================================================================================================
1. Independence of Observations
    Each row in the dataset represents a separate social media post.
    We assume that the views and engagement metrics of one post do not directly affect another post. Therefore, observations are considered independent.

2. No Perfect Multicollinearity
    Correlation analysis between engagement variables showed no perfect correlation (r ≠ 1).
    Therefore, predictors are not perfectly dependent on each other.

3. Continuous Dependent Variable
    The dependent variable (views) is numerical and continuous.
    Since views are measured in numbers (millions), they are suitable for correlation, ANOVA, and regression analysis.
